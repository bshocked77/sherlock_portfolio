About Me

Based in North London, I have been working professionally in television for 6 years across a range of productions. For the last 4 years, I've worked as a Senior Studio Technican at ITV Daytime, on shows such as Good Morning Britain, Lorraine, This Morning and Loose Women. I left ITV in January 2019 to persue a freelance career as a Self Shooting PD, where I ultimately would like to end up directing live events such as music or sports.

I am passionate about working on independent freelance projects to develop my skills. I can hire in any equipment needed but I also have my own kit allowing me to be self-sufficient. Currently, I am shooting on a Sony A7 iii, with a range of Zeiss lenses, a Mavic Air Drone, the latest Zhiyun Crane 3 LAB stabiliser, a GoPro Hero 7 and a radio mic kit to ensure the highest possible production values for sound when working with smaller budgets.

I pride myself on having great interpersonal skills, useful for conducting interviews and dealing with a range of talent. I am able to adapt daily depending on my role and team, and can also be a trusted self-shooter.
