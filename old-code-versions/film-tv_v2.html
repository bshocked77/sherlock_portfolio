Film & TV

Over the past decade I’ve worked on many productions, from my own personal projects, to national daytime shows, live events and musical productions. My role in all has always been varied and diverse, switching between the differing disciplines of sound, camera, lighting and scenic departments most notably for ITV as their Senior Studio Technician.
